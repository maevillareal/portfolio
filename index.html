Hi, I'm Mae!

Graphic and Web Designer

WITH EIGHT YEARS OF EXPERIENCE AND NOW SPECIALING IN REAL ESTATE GRAPHIC DESIGN, delivering minimalist yet visually striking designs.

My design philosophy is centered around simplicity and clean aesthetics. I believe that less is more and that effective communication can be achieved through thoughtful design. I take a fresh approach to each project, ensuring that my clients' vision is understood and delivered.

Apart from designing, I enjoy spending time outdoors, listening to music, and reading. There's nothing quite like a good hike or a relaxing stroll to clear the mind and spark new ideas. If you're looking for a designer with a diverse set of interests and a passion for their work, I'd be honored to collaborate with you and create something exceptional.
